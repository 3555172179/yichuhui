About Yichuhui

Your Trusted Partner in Commercial Refrigeration Since 2008

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Yichuhui (Zibo) Refrigeration Equipment Co., Ltd. has grown from a small local manufacturer into a leading supplier of commercial refrigeration equipment, serving customers across more than 50 countries worldwide.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, reliable refrigeration solutions at competitive prices. Over the years, we have expanded our product range, invested in advanced manufacturing technology, and built a team of skilled professionals dedicated to excellence.

Located in Zibo, Shandong Province – a hub of industrial innovation in China – our factory spans over 10,000 square meters and is equipped with state-of-the-art production lines and testing facilities.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, energy-efficient refrigeration solutions that exceed customer expectations and contribute to sustainable business operations worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted global partner for commercial refrigeration equipment, known for quality, reliability, and exceptional customer service.

Our Values

Quality, integrity, innovation, and customer satisfaction form the foundation of everything we do. We believe in building long-term relationships based on trust.
