Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ varies depending on the product type. For standard items, we can accept orders as low as 1 unit. For custom solutions, please contact us for specific requirements.

Do you offer OEM/ODM services?

Yes, we provide both OEM and ODM services. Our engineering team can work with you to develop custom refrigeration solutions tailored to your specific requirements and brand specifications.

What certifications do your products have?

Our products are certified with ISO 9001, CE, UL, and RoHS. We comply with international quality and safety standards to ensure our equipment meets regulatory requirements in your market.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a comprehensive 1-2 year warranty on all our products, depending on the type. Extended warranty options are available. Our warranty covers manufacturing defects and provides free replacement parts during the warranty period.

Do you provide installation and technical support?

Yes, we provide detailed installation manuals and video guides. For large projects, we can offer on-site installation support. Our technical team is available 24/7 to assist with any questions or issues.
